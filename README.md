# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

## 🔍 Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard built using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales, profit, and customer trends.
It provides insights into regional performance, product categories, and profitability to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## ❓ Business Questions

1. What is the overall sales, profit, and profit margin?
2. How are sales and profit trending over time?
3. Which regions generate the highest sales?
4. Which product categories and sub-categories perform best?
5. Which states are most profitable and which incur losses?
6. What are the top-performing products?

---

## ✅ Solutions / Insights

* **Overall Performance:**
  Total Sales: $2.28M, Total Profit: $283K, Profit Margin: 12.44%

*  **Trend Analysis:**
  Sales and profit show a steady upward trend from 2014 to 2018 with some fluctuations.

* **Regional Analysis:**
  West region contributes the highest sales, followed by East, Central, and South.

*  **Category Performance:**
  Technology generates the highest sales, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.

* **State-wise Insights:**
  Some states show high sales but negative profit, indicating cost or discount issues.

* **Product Insights:**
  A few sub-categories (like Phones and Chairs) drive a significant portion of revenue.
